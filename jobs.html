Overview

Dynamic Tech is a leading provider of comprehensive technology solutions for businesses. We operate across multiple areas, including software development, network infrastructure management, cybersecurity, cloud computing, data analytics, and artificial intelligence.

Company Information

  • Scale: 200+ employees, headquarters in Hanoi, with offices in Ho Chi Minh City and Da Nang
